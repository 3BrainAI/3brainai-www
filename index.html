What is an AI‑first Data Ops platform?

  • Ingests product data from feeds/ERP/PIM.
  • Normalizes & de‑duplicates titles, attributes, units, variants (EAN/SKU).
  • Enriches with classification/extraction & generation/localization models.
  • Validates with confidence‑gated, human‑in‑the‑loop review for borderline cases.
  • Publishes diffs to marketplaces with rollback.
  • Monitors buy‑box, completeness, DE‑coverage, and stock signals.

How it works (4 steps)

1) Ingestion

Schema/units detection, dedupe, lineage.

2) Core

Normalization, enum maps per channel, enrichment models.

3) BridgeSync

Diff‑based publish with rollback.

4) Monitoring

KPIs, alerts, buy‑box, DE‑coverage.
